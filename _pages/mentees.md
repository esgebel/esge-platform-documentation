If you are a Mentee, you will find this and related pages most useful.

## Getting started

### Creating an account

If it is the first time you participate in the GESEA educational programme, please go to the [register page](https://osp.gesea.eu/register).

1. Fill out your First name, Last name and email address, and choose a strong password.

2. Don't forget to check the box *I agree to the Terms of Service*.

3. Then simply click **Create Account**.

> You will receive an email with a link, click that link to verify your new account.

![signup-confirmation](/esge-platform-documentation/assets/signup-confirmation-email.png)

### First login

If your account was succesfully verified, you will be redirected to the login screen. Enter your email and password and then click **Sign in**.

When successful you will arrive at the **My Account** page, where you can setup your profile and account settings.

Initially there are several blocks visible opn this page:

- **Profile Information**: before you can continue to participate in training or certification sessions you will need to complete your profile information. Click **Edit Profile** to do this. Click **Save Changes** when you are happy.

- **Account Status**: This box shows how far you are in the completion of your profile. Until you have completed all information you will not be able to participate in Training and Certification sessions.

![profile-progress](/esge-platform-documentation/assets/complete-profile-progress.png)

- **Teachable LMS**: connect to Teachable to access your e-learning modules directly from the platform.

- **Account Security**: if you need to you can change your password here

- **Role Permissions**: A list of things you have access to on the platform

As soon as your profile is complete, you will unlock more functions.

### Completing your Profile

To start using the platform, complete your profile information:

1. On the **My Account** page click the button **Edit Profile**: a form will appear that you can fill out.
2. Skip the section **Assignment Details**, this will be completed by your mentor.
3. In the section **Mentee Profile** fill:
   - Gender
   - Date of Birth
   - Country
   - Dominant Hand
   - Affiliation: enter the institution you are affiliated with
   - Academic Level
   - Laparoscopy Basic: click the small (?) icon to view the explanation to select the correct option for your situation.
   - Laparoscopy Intermediate
   - Laparoscopy Advanced
   - Laparoscopy Expert
   - Hysteroscopy Basic
   - Hysteroscopy Intermediate
   - Hysteroscopy Advanced
   - Animal Model training
   - Trainer Box training
   - VR Training
   - Videogame hours/week
   - Music hours/week
4. When you are ready click **Save changes**

### Connect to Teachable

The Teachable e-learning courses are part of the GESEA Pathway, and you will need an ESGE membership before you have access to the e-learning modules. This membership can be registered for outside the OSP at the [ESGE Academy website](https://www.esge.org/membership-benefits/).

However, you can connect your OSP and Teachable account regardless:

1. Click the button **Connect to Teachable**
2. Choose either **Create new account** or **I already have an account**
3. If you chose **Create new account** the OSP will create an account in Teachable using the email address you signed up with at the OSP platform.
   3.1 Cick **Create Account** to confirm
4. If you chose **I already have an account** you will be asked to enter your Teachable credentials to confirm that you are the holder of this account
5. Once connected, the courses you need to complete and the progress will be displayed in your **My account** page.

### Request to be assigned to an Institution

Generally you will have to be linked to the institution where you are performing your Training or Certification sessions. Without being assigned to an institution, you can not take part in sessions. If your institution hasn't added your already, you can request this assignment yourself:

1. Click the button **Find institution**
2. Enter the name of the institution you want to connect with in the search bar, or just scroll through the list
3. For the institution you want to connect with, click the **Request** button.
4. Confirm the request by clicking the button **Send Request**
5. The pending request will be listed under **Your Connection Requests**, you will be notified as soon as the institution accepted the request.

![connection-request](/esge-platform-documentation/assets/connection-request.png)
